Payment Questions

You can make payments through the student portal after logging in. We accept:

  • Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
  • Apple Pay
  • PayPal
  • Bank transfers (BPAY)

Navigate to the "Pay Fees" section and follow the instructions to complete your payment.

Payment processing times vary by method:

  • Credit card/PayPal: Processed immediately
  • Apple Pay: Processed within 1 hour
  • Bank transfer (BPAY): 1-2 business days

You'll receive a confirmation email once your payment is processed.

Account Questions

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot password"
  3. Enter your Deakin email address
  4. Check your email for a reset link
  5. Follow the instructions to create a new password

If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder.

Payment history is only available to logged-in students. If you can't access it:

  • Make sure you're logged in with your Deakin credentials
  • Check that your browser isn't blocking cookies
  • Try clearing your cache and logging in again
  • If issues persist, contact our support team
